# Balfrin Minimal Demo vs Scientific Closure

The minimal demo contract is the smallest convincing Balfrin artifact for
scope control, reproducibility, and management review. It is satisfied when
the contract helper and dry-run planner are bounded, readable, and explicit
about inputs, commands, artifacts, evidence, visuals, success criteria, and
non-goals.

That is not scientific closure. Scientific closure still depends on the
post-run interpretation gate and measured evidence, and it remains separate
from any operational, annual-frequency, risk, exposure, vulnerability,
distributed-execution, or physical-probability claim.
